Delete Your GarageHunt Account

This page explains how to request deletion of your GarageHunt account and associated data. You do not need the app installed or to be logged in to make this request.

How to request deletion:
  1. Send an email to adambujold@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include the email address associated with your GarageHunt account
  3. We will confirm and process your deletion request within 30 days

adambujold@gmail.com

What gets deleted

DataWhat happens
Account profile (name, email, login credentials)Permanently deleted
Your listings, photos, saved searchesPermanently deleted
Your favorites, check-insPermanently deleted
Reviews/ratings you left about othersAnonymized and retained, to preserve the integrity of other users' ratings — no longer linked to your identity
Reviews/ratings others left about youAnonymized and retained for the same reason
Purchase/subscription recordsRetained as required for financial recordkeeping and tax compliance, disconnected from your deleted profile where possible

For full detail on how we handle your data, see our Privacy Policy.
